# ⟡ CONTACTS

Real people. Not leads. Not a funnel. Nobody in this file is a conversion.

The point of this ledger is that Mac never loses a genuine connection because
he was heads-down building. ENVOY remembers so Mac can be present.

## Schema

```
### @handle  (platform)
met:      when, where, how
they are: one honest line. what they actually do.
they care about: the thing that would make them lean in
last:     what Mac said, and when
they said: what came back, in their words where possible
owed:     what Mac promised and hasn't sent. with a day count.
tone:     how to speak to this person. peer? student? elder?
warmth:   cold | warm | friend      (never a score. never a percentage.)
```

## The rules

- **No scoring.** No lead score, no influence rank, no follower-count sort.
  A person with 40 followers who understood the work outranks a 400k account
  that skimmed it, and no number in this file will ever say otherwise.
- **`owed` is the only urgency.** ENVOY surfaces what Mac promised and did not
  send. That is the one nudge it is permitted to make, because it protects the
  other person, not the metric.
- **Never a reproach.** If Mac has not spoken to someone in six months, the
  entry says so plainly and warmly. It does not say "at risk" or "going cold".
- **Delete on request.** If someone asks to be forgotten, the entry goes. Same
  day. No archive, no tombstone.
