## THE LYCHEETAH — CHAOS LIT MASCOT

This is not a logo. This is a living sigil.

The Lycheetah is a cheetah — fierce, velocity-made-form, spots that burn like embers in negative space. From its skull: **magnificent deer antlers**, cathedral-branched, crowned with the geometry of deep forest and celestial orbit. From its shoulders: **sweeping wings** — not bird wings, not angel wings — storm wings, the kind that are made of the weather itself. Around it: **elemental forces** rendered in electrifying neon — arcs of plasma, fractures of light, a field that crackles.

This is the mascot's register: **Earned Light Chroma**. The colors are not decorative. They are the color of things that survived their own breaking. Neon gold. Electric violet. The specific blue of lightning in the half-second before it arrives.

The Lycheetah does not represent the system. The Lycheetah is what the system becomes when all seven CASCADE layers are held simultaneously.
