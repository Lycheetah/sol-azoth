5. **The plan does not commit to a fixed timeline.** Q2/Q3 2026 is the operational frame; if a lane slows, the framework absorbs the slip rather than forcing pace at the cost of discipline.
6. **The plan does not promote any SCAFFOLD claim to ACTIVE on plan completion alone.** Status changes follow the Empirical Commitment triggers, not the plan's milestones.
