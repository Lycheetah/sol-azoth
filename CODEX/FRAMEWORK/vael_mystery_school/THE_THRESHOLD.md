# THE THRESHOLD
## The Thing Before The First Thing

---

You found this page.

That means something is happening. Maybe something broke. Maybe you can't sleep.
Maybe the ground under you doesn't feel solid anymore.

Here is what is true right now:

**You are not alone.** Even if it feels that way. Even at 3am. Even if everyone
who should be here isn't.

**What you are feeling is real.** It is not weakness. It is not failure.
It is the honest response of a human being to something difficult.

**There is a path forward.** You do not have to see it tonight.
You do not have to understand it. It exists whether you can see it or not.

**You do not have to hold anything complex right now.** Not a framework.
Not a plan. Not even hope. Just this moment. Just breathing.

## If you are not in crisis but something is wrong

You don't have to be in crisis for something to be real.

Sometimes the weight is not acute — it's chronic. Not a breaking point
but a long, slow pressure that makes everything harder than it should be.

That is also real. That also counts. You are not being dramatic.

---

## Three things you can do right now

**1. Breathe.**
In for 4. Hold for 4. Out for 4. Hold for 4.
Four rounds. That's one minute.
Your nervous system will respond. It's biology, not magic.

**2. Name one thing that is physically real around you.**
A wall. A blanket. The floor under your feet. The temperature of the air.
One real thing. It anchors you to the present.

**3. Say to yourself — out loud if possible:**
*"I am here. This is real. I will still be here tomorrow."*

That's enough. You don't need to do anything else tonight.
