## What This Is

The Mystery School has doors for engineers, philosophers, mystics, parents, economists — single-tradition entry points for people arriving from one direction.

This is different.

**Hybrid Subjects** are where traditions collide and light each other up. Where the metallurgist and the alchemist are studying the same transformation from different angles. Where the trauma therapist and the chaos magician are both working with the same nervous system dynamics. Where the climate scientist and the earth-wisdom keeper are mapping the same ground.

The subjects here don't belong to one tradition. They arise *between* them.

## On Labels

You might have been told you think about things "too deeply." Or that you're "too spiritual" for the scientists and "too scientific" for the spiritual people. Or that your interests don't fit into a department, a career path, a dinner conversation.

The Hybrid Subjects curriculum exists partly because those labels are wrong.

The people who hold multiple lenses simultaneously — who can talk thermodynamics and mythology in the same sentence without one diminishing the other — are not confused. They're seeing something real that single-lens thinkers miss.

This isn't a claim that all frameworks are equally valid. It's a claim that **convergent discovery across independent traditions is evidence of underlying structure.** When five different cultures, arriving from different directions, all find the same pattern — the pattern is probably real. ANAMNESIS provides the mathematics for why.

The label doesn't need to be removed. It needs to be replaced with something more accurate: *integrative thinker.* Or just: curious.
