# WHERE AM I?
## A Self-Assessment for the Seven Phases

---

> *You don't need to know the mathematics to navigate.*
> *You just need to be honest about where you are.*

---

## HOW TO USE THIS

Read each section below. One will feel true — not the one you wish were true,
the one that actually describes your current experience. That's where you are.

There are no wrong answers. Every phase is necessary. Every phase has work to do
and gifts to offer. Nigredo is not worse than Rubedo — it's earlier in the cycle.
A seed in soil is not failing. It's beginning.

---

## THE ASSESSMENT

### Read each description. Score it 0-3:
- **0** = Not at all me right now
- **1** = Slightly true
- **2** = Mostly true
- **3** = Exactly where I am

---

- [ ] I feel like I need to rest but I'm not sure I've earned it
- [ ] The world feels both simple and empty

## COLOUR DEPTH CHECK

Now answer one more question:

**How deep does the current cycle feel?**

- [ ] **Surface level** — this is about a specific situation, project, or decision.
  → You're in **Citrinitas** (🟡) — light transformation. Growth and learning.

- [ ] **Medium depth** — this is about my relationships, my work, or how I live.
  → You're in **Albedo** (⬜) — moderate transformation. Refining and adjusting.

- [ ] **Deep** — this is about who I am, what my life means, or whether I want to be here.
  → You're in **Nigredo** (⚫) — deep transformation. Something fundamental is shifting.

- [ ] **Integrated** — I'm not in crisis or change. I'm building from a place of strength.
  → You're in **Rubedo** (🔴) — integration. Operating from completion.

## YOUR COORDINATES

Write this down:

**I am in Phase ___ at ___ depth.**

Example: "I am in Phase 3 (Insight) at Nigredo depth."

That means: you are seeing yourself with devastating clarity during a period
of fundamental change. That's one of the most painful — and most transformative —
positions in the entire cycle. It has a map. You are not lost.

Example: "I am in Phase 5 (Clarity) at Citrinitas depth."

That means: you are in sharp, productive flow while learning something new.
Enjoy it. Use it. Document what you create — you'll want it later.
