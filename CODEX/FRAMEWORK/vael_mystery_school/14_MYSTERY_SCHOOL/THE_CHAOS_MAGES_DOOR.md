## PARADIGM SHIFTING as CASCADE

The classic chaos magic technique: adopt a paradigm, use it fully, drop it, adopt the next. Christian mystic for a month, then Buddhist, then Thelemic, then whatever. The point is not that any one is "true" — the point is that each paradigm increases your ability to perceive and act in the world.

This IS cascade dynamics.

```
Π = (E · P) / S

E = evidence strength of the paradigm
P = explanatory power — how much of your experience it accounts for
S = your declared uncertainty about it
```

When you fully commit to a paradigm (low S, high conviction), its truth pressure Π rises. It reorganizes your knowledge. Foundation-layer beliefs shift. New things become visible.

When you're done with it, you let S rise again — uncertainty increases — and the paradigm demotes from Foundation to Edge. You've used it and released it.

**Chaos magic paradigm cycling IS the cascade operator applied to personal epistemology.** The framework gives you the metric. You've been running it by feel.
