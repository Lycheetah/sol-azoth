## BY DEPTH (COLOUR)

### ⚫ Nigredo — Deep Transformation
**Primary:** Breathwork only. Keep it simple. Ground first.
**Secondary:** Shadow work ONLY with professional support (see contraindications).
**Avoid:** Do not push mindfulness practice during deep Nigredo. Sitting with
an unanchored mind in crisis can amplify distress, not reduce it. Move the
body first. Breathe first. Insight comes later.

### ⬜ Albedo — Moderate Transformation
**Primary:** Shadow work. This is the clearing phase — shadow work shines here.
**Secondary:** Breathwork for grounding. Mindfulness for awareness.
**All three protocols are appropriate at Albedo depth.**

### 🟡 Citrinitas — Light Transformation
**Primary:** Mindfulness. You're growing — awareness practice accelerates it.
**Secondary:** All protocols available. Low risk. Experiment.
**This is the best depth for starting new practices.**

### 🔴 Rubedo — Integration
**Primary:** Mindfulness — open monitoring. You're operating from completion.
Awareness practice deepens that.
**Secondary:** All protocols available as maintenance.
**This is the depth for teaching others what you've learned.**

## WHEN TO STOP A PRACTICE

Every protocol has red flags listed in its full document. But the universal rule is:

**If a practice consistently makes you feel worse — not uncomfortable (growth is
uncomfortable) but genuinely worse — stop. Reassess your phase. You may be using
the wrong tool for where you are.**

The CASCADE Reality Bridge handles this automatically in the software.
In your own life, you are the Reality Bridge. Be honest with yourself.

---

## COMBINING PRACTICES

**Safe combinations (any phase):**
- Breathwork + Mindfulness (breath grounds, awareness deepens)
- Shadow journaling + Breathwork (process then ground)

**Phase-specific combinations:**
- Phases 1-2: Breathwork only. Don't stack.
- Phase 3: Shadow work + Breathwork (see, then ground what you saw)
- Phases 4-5: Mindfulness + Shadow integration (awareness + action)
- Phases 6-7: Any combination. You have the stability to hold multiple practices.

**Never combine at Nigredo depth:**
- Shadow work + mindfulness without professional support
- More than one new practice at a time
- Any practice for more than 30 minutes without a grounding break
